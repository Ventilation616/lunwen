# Abstract

n recent years, China has continuously advanced the national fitness strategy. The "Healthy China 2030" Planning Outline explicitly proposes to extensively carry out national fitness activities and promote the improvement of national health levels. However, traditional fitness guidance models have many limitations: professional fitness coach resources are unevenly distributed, with a significant gap in guidance service quality between first-tier cities and grassroots areas; offline personal training courses are expensive, making it difficult for ordinary fitness enthusiasts to afford long-term guidance costs; fitness venues are restricted by geographical location and time, unable to meet users' needs for exercising anytime and anywhere. Meanwhile, the rapid development of artificial intelligence technology has provided new solutions for intelligent fitness guidance. Breakthroughs in deep learning algorithms in the field of computer vision have made image-based pose recognition technology increasingly mature, capable of real-time capture of human keypoints and analysis of movement postures; the popularization of cloud computing and edge computing technologies has provided efficient and stable technical support for the storage, processing, and analysis of sports data; the rise of large language models has made intelligent movement guidance and error correction possible. Against this background, the sports movement correction system based on cloud collaboration and pose recognition algorithms has emerged, which has important theoretical significance and application value.

This paper researches and implements a sports movement correction system based on cloud collaboration and pose recognition algorithms. First, the development status and existing problems of intelligent fitness guidance are analyzed. Second, a sports movement correction system based on WeChat Mini Program and Python Flask is designed and implemented. Then, key functions of the system are implemented and optimized, including MediaPipe Pose pose recognition, OpenCV image preprocessing, and DeepSeek large language model integration. Finally, the effectiveness and feasibility of the system are verified through system testing. The research results show that the sports movement correction system proposed in this paper can effectively improve the intelligence level of fitness guidance, with push-up and sit-up movement recognition accuracy over 90%, average response time of 300 milliseconds, and support for 100 concurrent users, which has certain reference value in practical applications.

Keywords: Sports Movement Correction; Pose Recognition; Cloud Collaboration; MediaPipe; WeChat Mini Program

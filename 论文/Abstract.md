# Abstract

With the development of national fitness and artificial intelligence, problems in traditional fitness guidance such as scarce professional coaches, high costs, and venue limitations have become prominent. To solve the problem of limited fitness resources, this paper researches and implements a sports movement correction system based on cloud collaboration and pose recognition algorithms. First, the development status and existing problems of intelligent fitness guidance are analyzed. Second, a sports movement correction system based on WeChat Mini Program and Python Flask is designed and implemented. Then, key functions of the system are implemented and optimized, including MediaPipe Pose pose recognition, OpenCV image preprocessing, and DeepSeek large language model integration. Finally, the effectiveness and feasibility of the system are verified through system testing. The research results show that the sports movement correction system proposed in this paper can effectively improve the intelligence level of fitness guidance, with push-up and sit-up movement recognition accuracy over 90%, average response time of 300 milliseconds, and support for 100 concurrent users, which has certain reference value in practical applications.

Keywords: Sports Movement Correction; Pose Recognition; Cloud Collaboration; MediaPipe; WeChat Mini Program
